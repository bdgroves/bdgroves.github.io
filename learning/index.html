About This Section

How These Curricula Work

Each curriculum is a self-contained study path — lessons that expand with core concepts, vocabulary, and what I call the Cocktail Party Line: the one precise thing you can say at a dinner party that signals you actually understand the subject, not just the surface.

The goal is genuine comprehension. Not "I read a pop-science book about it" — more like "I could explain it to a physicist without embarrassing myself." That bar is higher than it sounds.

Ologies ↔ Learning

The Ologies section is where I build things — dashboards, pipelines, instruments. The Learning section is where I study the underlying science. They're related but different.

PELE tracks Kīlauea eruptions. The Volcanology curriculum explains why volcanoes work the way they do. SOLSTICE tracks the Casa Rinconada alignment. The Ancestral Puebloans curriculum explains who built it and why. The projects and the study inform each other.
