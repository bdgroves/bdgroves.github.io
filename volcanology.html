Lahar Hazard · Mt. Rainier · Puyallup Watershed
lahar-watch Live
Mount Rainier lahar hazard monitoring — the most lahar-prone volcano in North America sits 35 miles from Tacoma. Tracks USGS seismicity, snowpack, and river levels in the Puyallup, Carbon, and Nisqually drainages. The volcano you can see from the backyard.
bdgroves.github.io/lahar-watch ↗

SNOTEL and NASA MODIS snowpack data for the Mt. Rainier watershed — hourly updates, seasonal comparison, and melt rate tracking. The snowpack that feeds the rivers that lahars would follow.
