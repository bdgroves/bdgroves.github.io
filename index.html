Machine Learning · Paleontology · Nevada

🌊 PaleoWave — Fossil Finder

A Random Forest trained on the terrain signature of every known Triassic ichthyosaur locality in central Nevada, scoring where to dig next. Formation-stratified model, LiDAR-validated, 50 priority targets. They ruled the Triassic seas — this finds where they're buried.

View on GitHub →

Machine Learning · Megafauna · PNW

🦣 IceWave — Megafauna Intelligence

Split-ecoregion Random Forest predicting Pleistocene mammoth and mastodon fossil localities across five states. It called its shot: target E02 was ranked #1 before an active Columbian mammoth dig was confirmed at that exact pixel. Three methods, one answer.
